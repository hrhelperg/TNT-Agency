2. Services Provided

The Agency provides the following employment mediation services in accordance with Czech and applicable European Union law:

  • Direct placement (§ 14(1)(a)) — mediation of employment between employers and job seekers for permanent or fixed-term employment contracts.
  • Agency employment / temporary staffing (§ 14(1)(b)) — assigning employees to perform work for a user employer (agenturní zaměstnávání) in accordance with § 308–309 of Act No. 262/2006 Coll., the Labour Code.
  • Career counselling and outplacement (§ 14(1)(c)) — information and advisory services to job seekers.
  • Executive Search — confidential, direct-approach recruitment for senior management and C-level positions.
  • HR Consulting and Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) — advisory and outsourcing services relating to HR strategy, employer branding, and recruitment process design.

Specific deliverables, timelines, and fees for each engagement are agreed in a separate Service Agreement or Statement of Work signed by both parties. In case of conflict between a signed Service Agreement and these Terms, the Service Agreement prevails.

5. Replacement Guarantee
