1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognise your device on subsequent visits, remember your preferences, and provide us with information about how you use the site. Cookies may be set by the website itself ("first-party cookies") or by third-party services ("third-party cookies").

2. How We Use Cookies

TNT agency s.r.o. uses cookies solely for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies — necessary for the website to function correctly (e.g., your cookie consent preference). These cannot be disabled without impairing core functionality.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors interact with our site (pages visited, session duration, traffic sources). These are activated only after you explicitly accept analytics cookies via our consent banner.

We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting cookies, or social media tracking cookies.

4. Google Analytics 4 & Consent Mode v2

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) with Google Consent Mode v2. This means:

  • By default, analytics_storage, ad_storage, ad_user_data, and ad_personalization are all set to "denied" when you first visit our site.
  • No analytics cookies are set and no identifiable data is collected until you explicitly accept analytics cookies via our consent banner.
  • If you accept, Google Analytics may set the _ga and _ga_* cookies listed above to help us understand site traffic patterns and improve our website.

Google may process data on servers in the United States. This transfer is carried out under EU Standard Contractual Clauses. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage cookie preferences in the following ways:

  • Consent banner — when you first visit our site, you can accept or decline non-essential (analytics) cookies. You may revisit your choice at any time by clearing your browser's cookies and visiting the site again.
  • Browser settings — most browsers allow you to refuse, delete, or be notified about cookies. Please refer to your browser's help documentation:
    • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
    • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
    • Safari: Preferences → Privacy
    • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions
  • Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on — install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on to prevent your data from being used by Google Analytics on any website.

Please note that disabling or deleting analytics cookies will not affect the core functionality of our website. Blocking essential localStorage entries may affect the language-switching feature.

6. Third-Party Embeds

We do not currently embed third-party content (e.g., YouTube videos, social media widgets) that would set additional third-party cookies. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and our consent banner accordingly before such content goes live.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our website or the services we use evolve. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects any changes. For material changes that affect your consent choices, we will reset the consent banner so you can make a fresh choice.
